Tribute to Lionel Messi

.

Here is a timeline of Messi's Career:

  • 2004 - Messi signed his first professional contract on 4 February 2004. Messi made his first appearance in an official match on October 16, 2004.
  • 2005 - First La liga Trophy.
  • 2006 - First Champions league Trophy.
  • 2008 - Gold medal at Olymic Games
  • 2009 - First Ballon d'Or and Golden Shoe
  • 2012 - Barcelonas Top scorer and Record Breakng year scoring 91 goals in all competitions.
  • 2013 - Fourth Ballon d'Or in a row.
  • 2014 - All time top scorer in La Liga.
  • 2019 - 6th Ballon d'Or.
  • 2020 - Top Goalscorer with a Single Club Messi surpassed Brazilian legend Pele record for most goals with a single club.
  • 2021 - Tranfer to PSG and wins 7th Ballon d'Or.
  • 2022 - Wins the World Cup with Argentina and Finalissima Trophy.
  • 2023 - MLS & Inter Miami Transfer Lionel Messi signed for Major League Soccer club Inter Miami after leaving Paris Saint-Germain as a free agent.
  • 2023 - Wins 8th Ballon d'Or.
